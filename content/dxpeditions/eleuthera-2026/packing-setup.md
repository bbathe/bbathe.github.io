With departure approaching quickly, the equipment plan is finalized and everything has been assembled, powered, and is currently operating at home. The emphasis is on reliability, airline survivability, and the ability to operate both from the primary QTH and in the field without reconfiguring the core station.

## Why this setup
The IC-705 paired with the JUMA 1000 provides an excellent balance of portability and performance for travel based operating. The radio serves double duty as both the primary contest transceiver and the portable POTA rig, reducing redundancy while keeping the system simple. The JUMA 1000 adds the additional output needed for contest operation.

The multiband vertical covering 40m through 10m was selected to focus effort where it matters most for CQ WPX SSB, while remaining fast to deploy and tolerant of limited space. A single vertical with dedicated elements keeps setup time short, minimizes mechanical complexity, and allows quick band changes during the contest.

At this point, the remaining work is logistics rather than engineering.
