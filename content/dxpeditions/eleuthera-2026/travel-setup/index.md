I flew American Airlines from Cincinnati (**CVG**) with a stop in Miami (**MIA**) direct to Governor’s Harbour Airport (**GHB**). This means no messing with the Bahama domestic airlines.

The strategy for packing was to carry-on the IC-705 and JUMA PA-1000+, since they are the most "precious".  I also wanted to take advantage of the generous personal size carry-on that American Airlines allows, this means I only had to check (and pay for) one bag.  See [Packing & Setup]({{% ref "dxpeditions/eleuthera-2026/packing-setup" %}}) for all the details.

The sum total was approiximately 100 lbs of gear.

![Luggage](luggage.jpeg)

All the flights went smooth and the checked luggage made it thru all the stops.  TSA at CVG, and Customs at GHB really struggled to undersatnd what I was doing.  In particular, they were concerned about the amp but eventually gave up and let me go thru.

The cottage I am operating out of was in Rock Sound, which is about an hour drive from GHB.  There are no major car rental companies operating out of Eleuthera, so you end up renting from local companies.  I stopped on the way and bought some groceries, and made it to the cottage by 2pm local (1800 UTC).

Setup went smooth, and I was one the air by 1930 UTC.  Here are the obligatory antenna and station photos.
